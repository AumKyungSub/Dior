OUR MISSION

Our mission, to only leave beauty as a legacy, means being fully committed to
reducing our carbon emissions by 46% full scope by 2030 and aligning our
business with the 1.5°C pathway. It also means to gather our efforts in favor of
biodiversity with flower farming as driver of regeneration. At the heart of
everything we do lies the limitless love of flowers that inspired Monsieur Dior.

To craft Parfums Christian Dior's sustainability vision and strategy, we worked
from the ground up by consulting a wide panel of internal and external
stakeholders, including experts, clients, NGOs, students... It serves as our guide
as we set out to breathe new life into our approach to sustainability. Our strategy
is built upon 5 pillars. Our commitments and actions are detailed hereafter.
